# Chapter 1: The Opening

*When an aesthetic encounter begins, it does not begin with a decision. It begins with a summons.*

## Draft Notes

- Begin with the phenomenon of being addressed
- Stofftrieb (material drive) — the world presses in, we are not neutral observers
- Zeami's kizen — the sudden arising of the aesthetic moment
- Personal narrative: the first time I recognized I was being addressed, not merely informed
- Not passive reception but active submission to the encounter
- The opening is always already underway — we never start from zero
